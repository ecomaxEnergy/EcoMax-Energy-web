WHO WE ARE

We are passionate entrepreneurs dedicated to generating green energy solutions for India. Our team is driven by a shared vision of sustainability and innovation, striving to transform biomass residues into clean, renewable energy. With a deep commitment to environmental stewardship, we focus on developing cutting-edge technologies that reduce reliance on fossil fuels.

Our expertise spans across structured finance, renewable energy, and infrastructure projects, enabling us to deliver scalable and impactful solutions. At EcoMax Energy, we believe in empowering communities through sustainable development, ensuring a greener future for generations to come. Together, we are paving the way for energy independence and a healthier planet.

OUR VISION

"To harness India's abundant biomass residues to create sustainable and renewable energy solutions, empowering our nation towards energy independence and environmental stewardship."

We believe in leveraging India's natural resources to solve its energy challenges. By innovating and developing cutting-edge technologies that convert biomass residues into clean energy.

OUR MISSION

"Our mission is to transform India's biomass residues into clean, renewable energy, driving sustainable development and contributing to energy security."

We are dedicated to innovation, excellence, and collaboration to deliver environmentally responsible energy solutions that empower communities and protect our planet for future generations."

FOUNDER

Rithvik Rao Madhavarapu

Managing Director

Rithvik Rao Madhavarapu is a seasoned investment banking professional with extensive experience across various sectors and geographies. At a prominent London-based investment bank, he managed a structured finance portfolio, including project finance deals for wind farms, solar projects, and infrastructure. Previously, at Avantage Reply, he consulted for top investment banks. He also served as Assistant Vice President of Finance for Nandi Grain Derivatives and worked at Kantar Consulting in Boston.
